## CONFIRMED (host-verified where marked *)

- **C1* [ROBUSTNESS] Acceptance gates admit `Solved_To_Acceptable_Level`.**
  `sundman_homotopy.m:47` / `minfuel_at_tf.m:84` gate on
  `success && maxDefect<1e-6`; `success` includes acceptable-level, letting
  1e-5-grade duals into warm chains and PMP checks. 3-way convergent.
  **FIXED 2026-07-21**: gates now require `Solve_Succeeded`.
- **C2* [METHODOLOGY] `certified` does not require the requested homotopy
  endpoint.** `certified = ~isempty(best)` — an eps>0 best can carry the
  flag. **FIXED 2026-07-21**: certified now requires the final scheduled
  eps (and `best.epsReached` is stored), matching the earth campaign's
  stricter semantics.
- **C3 [METHODOLOGY] Switch counts are exact-integer nodal counts** with no
  mesh-refinement band anywhere (flagship included); `certify_minfuel_pmp`
  additionally demands a strict integer PMP-crossing match that can fail
  spuriously on node-grazing switches. OPEN -> TODO (mesh-band study).
- **C4 [ROBUSTNESS] No bound-saturation diagnostic**; velocity boxes
  [-12,12] sit near the ~9.9 ND perigee scale. **FIXED 2026-07-21**
  (ladder-prep T3): `out.boundSat` + warning; opt-in `vBox/rBox` widening.
- **C5 [LADDER] Thrust is campaign-global** (`minfuel_config`), min-time
  `cBox`/`tfCapMult` fixed, `tauf0` frozen across solves — a T_max ladder
  is not parameterized end-to-end and will go structurally infeasible at
  deep rungs (the earth campaign's rung-adaptive-dL lesson, L6).
  **ADDRESSED 2026-07-21** (ladder-prep): thrust parameterized, fingerprints
  + `chain_rung_seed_tulip` land the ladder infrastructure. **But the 20 mN
  pilot EMPIRICALLY confirmed the fixed-τf topology wall** at a single 20%
  rung (winding can't grow) — the tulip ladder needs a free-span
  reformulation, not just the prep. See `process/LADDER_PREP_PILOT_FINDINGS.md`.
- **C6 [ROBUSTNESS] `gen_tulip_energy_2p` step_solve lacks the rF fallback**
  after a failed tight solve (both models flag the same class in elfo).
  **FIXED 2026-07-21** (ladder-prep T5).

  steering-law regeneration, NOT Gemini's time-interp fix (which still
  aliases across rev counts).
